What is the angular displacement?
The angular displacement of a specific body is the angle through which a point revolves around a center or a specific axis in a specified sense.
How to calculate angular displacement?
To calculate angular displacement you should use the following formula:
s = r * Φ
where:
s = arc length
r = distance
Φ = angular displacement
Why use angular displacement?
Angular displacement is used in order to measure the circular path velocity and acceleration.
